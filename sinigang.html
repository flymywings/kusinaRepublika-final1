Sinigang na Baboy is a classic Filipino sour soup known for its tangy tamarind flavor. Tender pork pieces are simmered with fresh vegetables like kangkong, radish, eggplant, and string beans in a savory-sour broth that‚Äôs perfect with steamed rice. Comforting, healthy, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pack tamarind soup base mix or fresh tamarind
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 100g kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 cup string beans
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced
  • 2‚Äì3 liters water
  • Fish sauce and salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pork with onions and tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add tamarind mix or fresh tamarind and simmer.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Season with fish sauce and salt.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
